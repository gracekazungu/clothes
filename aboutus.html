AFFORDABLE CLOTHES SHOP

Affordable Second Hand Clothes
is a popular chain of retail shops that offers quality and stylish second-hand clothing
at affordable prices and unrivaled shopping experience.





WHAT WE BELIEVE

Am proud to support the circular economy model in the fashion industry
by giving new life to second-hand clothing while providing a
welcoming experience to our customers.




MY VISION

I aim to be at the forefront of the circular economy model
by enabling the re-use of second-hand clothes.
In doing so, we lower carbon emissions and assist in the fight against climate change.
The re-use of clothes also helps to mitigate other environmental
concerns by reducing excessive consumption of brand new clothes that cause an additional strain on resources.
